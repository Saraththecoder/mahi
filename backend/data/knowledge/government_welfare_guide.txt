Agricultural Government Schemes Guide:
1. PM-KISAN: Rs. 6,000 annual income support to all landholding farmer families in three equal installments.
2. PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana): Low premium crop insurance scheme (2% Kharif, 1.5% Rabi).
3. Kisan Credit Card (KCC): Provides concessionary interest rate loans to farmers with 3% subvention for timely repayment.
4. Soil Health Card Scheme: Promotes balanced fertilizer usage by giving free soil nutrient analysis every 2 years.
