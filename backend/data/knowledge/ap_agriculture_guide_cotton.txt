Cotton Cultivation Guidelines (Andhra Pradesh Agriculture Department):
Cotton thrives in deep black soils and well-drained loamy soils.
Sowing time: June to July. Recommended spacing: 90x60 cm for hybrids.
Fertilizer dose: 120-60-60 kg NPK per hectare. Apply nitrogen in three split doses.
Common pests: Pink Bollworm, sucking pests (Jassids, Aphids).
Pest management: Intercrop with cowpea or maize, use pheromone traps (5 per acre).
